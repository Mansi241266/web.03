Science Exhibition

The Science Exhibition is a platform for school students to showcase their creativity, innovation, and problem-solving skills through working models, experiments, and projects. Students will present their ideas on themes like sustainability, renewable energy, robotics, and AI.

Date: October 17, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Venue: School of Computing, Exhibition Hall

Rules & Eligibility

  • Open to school students (Grades 8–12).
  • Projects can be individual or team-based (max 3 participants).
  • Each team must bring their own materials and setup.
  • Judging will be based on creativity, innovation, and presentation.
  • Decision of judges will be final.
