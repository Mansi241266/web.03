Coding Competition

The Coding Competition is designed for school students to test their problem-solving abilities, logical thinking, and coding skills. Participants will solve algorithmic challenges in real-time and showcase their programming talent.

Date: October 17, 2025

Time: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM

Venue: School of Computing, Lab 1

Rules & Eligibility

  • Open to school students (Grades 9–12).
  • Individual participation only (no teams).
  • Languages allowed: C, C++, Java, Python.
  • Problems will range from beginner to intermediate level.
  • Use of internet or external material is not permitted.
  • Judging will be based on accuracy and time efficiency.
