Capture the Flag (CTF)

The Capture the Flag competition is a cybersecurity contest where college students solve challenges related to cryptography, web exploitation, reverse engineering, and digital forensics. The goal is to “capture flags” (hidden codes) within challenges to earn points. It’s the ultimate test of hacking skills, logic, and speed.

Date: October 18, 2025

Time: 9:30 AM – 2:00 PM

Venue: Cybersecurity Lab, School of Computing

Rules & Eligibility

  • Open to college students only.
  • Team size: 2–3 members.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops.
  • Internet access will be restricted to CTF platform only.
  • External collaboration or cheating will lead to disqualification.
  • Scoring is based on the number of flags captured and time taken.
