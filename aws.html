AWS Study Jam

The AWS Study Jam is a cloud computing bootcamp for college students. Participants will explore Amazon Web Services (AWS) through guided labs, hands-on sessions, and real-world use cases. By the end of the workshop, students will have practical knowledge of cloud infrastructure, serverless computing, and DevOps practices.
