Workshop

The Workshop for school students will provide hands-on learning sessions on trending technologies such as robotics, IoT, web development, and AI. The goal is to spark curiosity and provide practical exposure beyond textbooks.

Date: October 18, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Venue: School of Computing, Lab 2

Rules & Eligibility

  • Open to school students (Grades 8–12).
  • Participants must bring their own laptops (if required).
  • Basic knowledge of computers is expected.
  • All workshop materials will be provided on-site.
  • Certificates will be awarded to participants.
